Relative Value Assessment · Key Findings

Positive Deviation Opportunity: Long22 CB is the only bond with a straight bond value above par (98.52) and a conversion premium below 20%, offering a significant relative value advantage. Its AAA rating and ample liquidity make it the top choice for the core portfolio foundation.

Watch Signal: Both Tong22 CB and JingAo CB have conversion premiums in the 24-29% range, with valuations at industry median levels. However, Tong22's reset trigger condition of 15/30, 80% (more lenient than Long22's 85%) means that if the board initiates a reset process, the conversion premium could quickly compress below 10%.

Risk Zone: Both Tian23 CB (35.2%) and JingNeng CB (42.1%) are deep out-of-the-money, with conversion premiums significantly above the industry average (25-30%). Their credit spreads are also elevated, reflecting the market's dual concerns over continued stock price declines and credit risk.

Implied Volatility Insight: JingNeng CB's implied volatility of 41.3% is significantly higher than Long22's 32.5%, indicating the market expects higher future stock volatility for JingNeng. However, high volatility needs to be paired with a directional view on the underlying stock — if the stock continues to decline, high volatility offers no protection.

7. Methodology Notes

Four-Dimension Scoring Method

Relative Value (Weight 30%): Straight Bond Value (40%) + Conversion Premium (30%) + Credit Spread (30%)

Covenant Protection (Weight 25%): Reset Covenant (40%) + Put Protection (25%) + Call Covenant (25%) + Other (10%)

Liquidity (Weight 25%): Avg Daily Volume (40%) + Turnover Rate (20%) + Bid-Ask Spread (25%) + Block Trade Feasibility (15%)

Credit Quality (Weight 20%): Issuer Rating (40%) + Debt Rating (30%) + Stock Quality (30%)

Scoring Standards: Each dimension scored out of 10, weighted sum gives composite score (out of 10). Top 2 bonds (score > 6.5) recommended for standard or overweight allocation. Bonds scoring 4.5-6.5 recommended for underweight or trading primarily. Bonds scoring < 4.5 recommended for avoidance.

Key Indicator Formulas

Straight Bond Value: Sum of present values of future coupon and principal discounted at same-tenor treasury yield + credit spread

Conversion Premium: (CB Price - Conversion Value) / Conversion Value x 100%

Conversion Value: 100 / Conversion Price x Underlying Stock Price

Straight Bond YTM: Yield to maturity that equates the present value of future bond cash flows to the straight bond value (i.e., excluding conversion option value)

Credit Spread: Straight Bond YTM - Same-tenor Treasury Bond Yield to Maturity

Implied Volatility: Volatility parameter derived from BS model inversion by decomposing CB price into straight bond value + conversion option value

Bid-Ask Spread: (Best Ask - Best Bid) / Mid Price x 10000 (unit: bp)

Impact Cost: Deviation between actual average execution price and pre-order mid price for a given trade size (unit: bp)
